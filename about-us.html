About us

MPower Steel company Ltd. was incorporated as a Ugandan company in the year 2020. MPower Steel is part of MPower group of companies having interests in the core sectors of Energy, Power, Steel etcetera, in Africa and Asia.

MPower Steel though being the groups brand new addition has already been gaining traction in the business circles in the region riding on the success of group company MPower Oil, Uganda.

MPower Oil Company Ltd., Uganda recently took over the businesses of Mineral Oil Company Ltd., Uganda. Mpower Oil is now the leading manufacturer, distributor and exporter of Automotive and Industrials Lubricants in the region. MPower Oil has presence in the form of local offices in all major business districts and has distributorships in almost all major cities and towns of the region.

Kampala City, Uganda

Victoria Lake, Kampala, Uganda

Mpower Steel is located in Kampala, Uganda's national and commercial capital. Kampala is famous for red-tiled villas in the midst of tree covered hills. These hills surround an urban centre of contemporary skyscrapers that serve as the preferred place for the businesses in the city. The city's downtown houses the Uganda Museum. The museum explores the country's rich tribal heritage through an extensive collection of artefacts.

The other prime attraction of the city is Lake Victoria also called Victoria Nyanza. It's the largest of the African Great Lakes. Lake Victoria is also the principal source of the River Nile, one of the longest and most important rivers flowing to the Mediterranean Sea. Spending time at dusk and dawn time around Lake Victoria is a source of pure bliss for both visitors and the local population.
