About us

MPower Steel company Ltd. was incorporated as a Ugandan company in the year 2020. MPower Steel is part of MPower group of companies having interests in the core sectors of Energy, Power, Steel etcetera, in Africa and Asia.

MPower Steel though being the groups brand new addition has already been gaining traction in the business circles in the region riding on the success of group company MPower Oil, Uganda.

MPower Oil Company Ltd., Uganda recently took over the businesses of Mineral Oil Company Ltd., Uganda. Mpower Oil is now the leading manufacturer, distributor and exporter of Automotive and Industrials Lubricants in the region. MPower Oil has presence in the form of local offices in all major business districts and has distributorships in almost all major cities and towns of the region.

Our Vision

Best Steel

To bring all the latest Steel manufacturing technologies available globally and make the best Steel. ‘Best Steel’ means Steel that has a technologically superior composition, is economically efficient and environmentally sustainable to produce.


Build Economies

MPower Steel plans to source, produce, manufacture and sell; products and services; in local as well as in international markets. MPower will contribute towards equitable development and economic growth of not only the region but of the entire world.


Better life

MPower will strive to; provide equal opportunities of work and equitable access to education to the people; create a world free of poverty and hunger; elevate the lives of the people in general; and contribute to progress of the human society as a whole.
